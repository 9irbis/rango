Rango, a place to categorize webpages

Rango is a spanish word meaning class, degree or rank. True to its name, this website allows registered users to categorize webpages based on what topic they are about. Each category has a list of web pages associated with it which are ranked according to the number of times they are viewed. The categories themselves are ranked according to their number of likes.
